Spiritual Wisdom: Hindi Quotes is a quote-reading and sharing app. The app provides Hindi and English spiritual wisdom quote cards, categories, favorites, save/share features, and a mantra section.

This app does not require users to create an account or log in.

1. Information stored locally on your device

The app stores some information locally on your device to provide basic app features. This may include:

  • Favorite quotes
  • Simple usage counts or app preferences
  • Selected quote card theme
  • Your optional name for quote cards, if you choose to enter it

This locally stored information is not sent to our own server. You can remove it by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

2. Sharing and saving quote cards

If you choose to share a quote card, sharing is handled through your device's share sheet. You choose where to send the content.

If you choose to save a quote card, the app may save the generated image to your device gallery or storage. The app does not upload your photos, files, or saved quote cards to our own server.

4. Data sharing

The app does not sell your personal data. However, data may be shared with Google through the Google Mobile Ads SDK for advertising, analytics, diagnostics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

5. Data security

Data transmitted by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit. Local app preferences and favorites are stored on your device.

6. Children

This app is not designed specifically for children. It is intended for a general audience interested in spiritual wisdom, Hindi quotes, good morning quote cards, suvichar, bhakti, peace, motivation, and mantra practice.

7. Accounts and data deletion

This app does not allow users to create an account and does not provide an account login system.

Because the app does not maintain a user account database on our own server, there is no account deletion request system. You can delete locally stored app data by clearing the app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app.
